#### OpenLLM - open source platform designed to simplify the deployment, management, and operation of large language models in production environment.
    OpenLLM is focused on production deployment - it's designed to streamline the deployment and operation of LLMs in production environments. Key characteristics:
        •  Deployment-focused: Features a simplified workflow for creating enterprise-grade cloud deployment with Docker, Kubernetes, and BentoCloud
        •  BentoML ecosystem: Provides functionalities to fine-tune, serve, deploy, and monitor LLMs with ease
        •  Production workflow: Package models into "Bentos" (standardized units) for containerization and cloud deployment
        •  Enterprise features: Supports deployment via BentoCloud, an AI inference platform with autoscaling and model orchestration
        •  Integration-rich: Integrates with LangChain, LlamaIndex, BentoML, and Hugging Face

#### https://aaif.io/ - Agentic AI Foundation. New, under the parent umbrella of the Linux Foundation.

The AAIF was started by a heavyweight group of "founding platinum members" ($350,000): AWS, Anthropic, Block, Bloomberg, Cloudflare, Google, Microsoft, and OpenAI. 
The stated goal is to provide "a neutral, open foundation to ensure agentic AI evolves transparently and collaboratively".
Anthropic have donated **Model Context Protocol** to the new foundation, OpenAI donated **AGENTS.md**, Block donated **goose** (their open source, extensible AI agent).
